A Personal Journey

With a background in smart hardware and AI at major tech companies, I'm now exploring new frontiers in AI entrepreneurship.

Life took an unexpected turn when my wife was diagnosed with thyroid nodules during a routine check-up two years ago. This year, a biopsy revealed a malignant tumor, leading to surgery to remove her left thyroid. While the surgery was successful, it marked the beginning of a new chapter in our lives, including careful dietary management - particularly with foods like seaweed and cruciferous vegetables.

This personal experience sparked an idea. Initially considering a diet tracking app (though spreadsheets proved sufficient), I wanted to help with post-surgery neck stiffness. Inspired by my wife's love for match-3 games and my passion for Tetris, plus seeing an AirPods-based neck posture app on Product Hunt, everything clicked!

The magic of Neck Blocks lies in its ability to transform essential exercises into fun - just 10 minutes can make your whole day better. While it started as a tool for post-thyroid surgery recovery, it's become valuable for anyone dealing with neck tension, especially office workers at risk of cervical issues (like myself).

Building on an existing blocks project, I've added several innovative features:

  • AirPods integration for motion control (tilt up to rotate, down to speed up, with intuitive left-right movements)
  • A unique match-3 mode (combining the best of both worlds - with plans for themed graphics in future updates)
  • Disabled operation sound effects
  • More exciting features in development - stay tuned!

Looking ahead, I'm excited about developing an immersive adventure game using spatial audio and AirPods head tracking. Imagine navigating through a world where monsters lurk to your left and treasures beckon from the right, using just your hearing and head movements to guide you.

It's a whole new way to game - no visual input, just audio and motion. Intrigued? Let's connect and explore these possibilities together!
