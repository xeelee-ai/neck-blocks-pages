Developer's Story

I previously worked in smart hardware and AI at large companies, and now I'm venturing into AI-related entrepreneurship.

Two years ago, during a routine physical examination, my wife was diagnosed with thyroid nodules. This year, after a biopsy, we found it was a malignant tumor, so she underwent surgery to remove the left thyroid. The surgery went well, but it was quite a long process. Going forward, she needs to be careful with her diet—for example, limiting foods like kelp and cruciferous vegetables.

This inspired me to create an application (initially a diet control app, but I found online spreadsheets could solve that problem) to help her recover from neck stiffness. Knowing she loves match-3 games and I love Tetris, and after seeing an app on PH that reminds you about neck bending time using AirPods, I had an aha moment!

So I modified a blocks project and added the following features:

  • Support for playing games through head movement detection with AirPods (tilt up to rotate blocks, tilt down to accelerate block falling, left and right movements are self-explanatory)
  • Support for match-3 mode (wanted both features, that's just how it is; originally wanted to add vegetable and fruit textures, but that'll have to come later)
  • Turned off some operation sound effects because I prefer using headphones with noise cancellation, either without playing anything or with soft music or yoga audio

This also inspired me to develop a future adventure game using spatial audio and AirPods head control—essentially, a game you play using only your ears and head movements, not your eyes. If you're interested in this concept, please reach out to discuss and explore together!

开发者故事

我曾在大型公司從事智慧硬體和人工智慧領域的工作，現在正在涉足與人工智慧相關的創業。

兩年前，在一次例行體檢中，我妻子被診斷出患有甲狀腺結節。今年，經過活檢，我們發現這是一個惡性腫瘤，於是她接受了手術，切除了左側甲狀腺。手術很順利，但這是一個相當漫長的過程。今後，她需要仔細注意飲食——例如，限制海帶和十字花科蔬菜等食物。

這啟發我創建了一個應用程式（最早是飲食控制的APP，但我發現線上表格就能解決該問題），來幫助她恢復僵硬的頸部。想到她喜歡消消樂，我喜歡俄羅斯方塊，然後看到PH上有個通過AirPods來提醒頸部彎曲時間的APP，靈光一閃，啊哈！

主要功能：

  • 支援通過AirPods來檢測頭部動作來進行遊戲（抬頭為旋轉方塊，低頭加速方塊下落，左右就不用說了）
  • 支援消消樂模式（既要又要，就是要這樣，本來還想加上蔬果的貼圖，只能後續了）
  • 把一些操作的音效關閉了，因為我喜歡耳機就開降噪，不播放東西，或者播放柔和一些輕音樂或者瑜伽音頻

Developer's Story

I previously worked in smart hardware and AI at large organisations, and now I'm venturing into AI-related entrepreneurship.

Two years ago, during a routine physical examination, my wife was diagnosed with thyroid nodules. This year, after a biopsy, we discovered it was a malignant tumour, so she underwent surgery to remove the left thyroid. The surgery went well, but it was quite a lengthy process. Going forward, she needs to be rather careful with her diet—for example, limiting foods such as kelp and cruciferous vegetables.

This inspired me to create an application (initially a diet control app, though I realised online spreadsheets could solve that particular issue) to help her recover from neck stiffness. Knowing she loves match-3 games and I'm rather fond of Tetris, and after seeing an app on PH that reminds one about neck bending time using AirPods, I had rather a brilliant idea!

So I modified a blocks project and added the following features:

  • Support for playing games through head movement detection with AirPods (tilt upwards to rotate blocks, tilt downwards to accelerate block falling, whilst left and right movements are rather self-explanatory)
  • Support for match-3 mode (wanted both features, that's simply how it is; originally wanted to add vegetable and fruit textures, though that shall have to come later)
  • Turned off certain operation sound effects as I prefer using headphones with noise cancellation, either without playing anything or with rather soft music or yoga audio

This has also inspired me to develop a future adventure game using spatial audio and AirPods head control—essentially, a game one plays using only one's ears and head movements, rather than one's eyes. Should you be interested in this concept, do reach out to discuss and explore together!

主要功能：

  • 支援通過AirPods來檢測頭部動作來進行遊戲（抬頭為旋轉方塊，低頭加速方塊下落，左右就不用說了）
  • 支援消消樂模式（既要又要，就是要這樣，本來還想加上蔬果的貼圖，只能後續了）
